# Notes on Prompting


## Various Superprompts

### Rants to op-ed
```text
Please turn the following text into an op-ed in the New York Times. The vocabulary should be simple and not ornate. It should be concise and  accessible to an advanced high-schooler whenever possible.

Please format it in Markdown.
```

### Restyle paragraph
```text
Please rephrase the following text into a paragraph in an op-ed in the New York Times. The vocabulary should be simple and not ornate. It should be concise and  accessible to an advanced high-schooler whenever possible.

Please format it in Markdown.
```
